My Project and inspiration for this site.

Well it was originally a love of nightrider mixed with a dream to own a quirky car and a car that really would stand out!. Problem is a Pontiac firebird circa the late eighties early ninties is frankly a ball of rubbish. Since its maiden voyage in a local St patrick's day festival its been stuck nearly literally in a garage providing a home to cats and mice and basically taking up any useable space in the garage with the honest belief that ill get around to fixing the bloody thing. I say bloody because lets be honest the red looks better than nightrider black and i really cant put a red flashing light across the front of it no matter how criminal the car has perfromed since purchased. Plus any connection to the "Hoff" no matter how cool i think it is would be considered poor judgement. So as you can see in the before and hopefully after pictures i've decided to go down the traditional route and make the car hopefully look half decent.
